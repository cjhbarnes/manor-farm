The Tearooms

Centered around our traditional Farm Court Yard you will find the Stable Tea Rooms, adjacent to our Farm Shop.

The Tea Rooms are open everyday from 9:30 in the morning, closing at 5:30 during the week, and at 5:00 at the weekends.

Breakfasts, lunches and teas are available everyday, with a large and varied homemade menu which changes daily. A great opportunity for visitors to try our home produced meat and produce.

Hand Made Cakes

Fabulous home-made scones and cakes, with whole cakes being available to order and take home, and other take away meals available. Our local ice cream is made by Lovingtons, hand mixed in Somerset, in a large range of flavours, a true taste of the West!

Special Events

The new extension to the Stable Tea rooms in now open for business. This resulted from a expansion to the old feed store in the courtyard. This can be used for private hire for large parties, corporate meetings and family gatherings otherwise you can just meet with friends for a cooked breakfast, light lunch or a delicious homemade cream tea. You will always get a warm welcome and excellent service. This room for up to 120 people to be seated in comfort indoors. In addition there is outside space with tables and chairs for the warmer weather.

New luxury accessible toilets with easy access right to the door makes for a welcome place to visit whatever your mobility.

Ample parking and coaches welcome by appointment.
